Studying Tips for Struggling Computing Students

October 1, 2021 by Casey Willis

Whether you enroll in a Bachelor's or a Master's degree in Computer Science, you want to be a top student and prove that you have advanced your computing skills. Regardless of your specialization, Computer Science degrees are challenging, even if you are a tech fanatic and passionate about Computer Science. So how should you deal with all the work and information that you procure throughout a single day? Here are some basic tips and tricks to keep in mind if you want to be a successful Computer Science student, if not, the best in your class:

  • 1. Don't expect to learn everything during lectures
  • 2. Studying the night before an exam will NOT work
  • 3. Focus on developing your practical Computer Science skills
  • 4. Don't be a lone wolf
  • 5. Remember:You still have a life
